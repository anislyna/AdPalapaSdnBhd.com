About Us



AD Palapa Sdn. Bhd. is the name of the company; It was known as DWAM Enterprise in 2011. In 2022, AD Palapa Sdn. Bhd. has upgraded to a private limited company. From only an agent to supply workers and provide cleaning services when it was established over a decade ago, AD Palapa Sdn. Bhd. is currently involved in the business supplying local and foreign workers in security services industry and environmental industry. The company is located in Sungai Besi, Kuala Lumpur.



Dewi Aryani
(MANAGING DIRECTOR)





Mrs. Dewi Aryani, a Malaysian, is the founder and managing director. As our Managing Director, she is responsible for developing the overall strategic direction of our company and has been instrumental in its growth.

She previously worked in the security services industry as General Manager (Operation) in a security company at Bangi, Selangor since 2000. She is responsible for finding good security guards from various aspects to keep the client's area & building safe as the General Manager (Operations). In 2011, she started a business to provide workers to the former company by providing security guards and cleaning services at office.

Services



      Building Cleaning:


Building maintenance refers to activities performed to retain and restore the functionality of resdential and commercial properties. It includes tasks such as cleaning, landscaping, and electrical system maintenance. It aims to preserve a safe, functional, and comfortable environment for tenants at all the time.



      Office Cleaning:


For ongoing regular cleaning, commercial cleaners will sweep hard floors, mop with a disinfect. They will vacuum carpets and do a light dusting surfaces. They will also clean bathrooms and kitchens, incuding sinks, tilets, faucets, light fixtures, and high touchpoints like doorknobs and light swtiches.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What days and hours can I have building and office cleaned?

    Our office teams are available during normal working hours as well as early morning, evening and Saturdays.

  • Is your work guaranteed?

    Totally! In the unlikely event you are not absolutely satisfied, please contact us within two working days, and we’ll either re-perform that service or we may provide you with a full or partial refund. Your choice.

  • If there is a problem, who can I call?

    Our reputation depends on our exceptional standards which we take very seriously. However in the unlikely event of any concern, please call us on 03- 9054 4836, or email us at adpalapa6571@gmail.com, and we will do our utmost to resolve it immediately.

  • How can I give you feedback?

    Our commitment to you is the cornerstone of our company. So we would be delighted to hear from you, by phone, or by email or letter, or we’re happy to come and meet you. Of course you can also fill in the comments card form we leave after each visit.

  • Cancellation

    Please let us know as far in advance as possible. If we cannot gain access to your home or office, or if we have no or less than 24 hours’ notice, we may be obliged to charge the full price. For cancellations between 24 and 48 hours’ notice, we may charge up to half of the fee. The reason for this is we are unable to re-assign the team on such short notice.
